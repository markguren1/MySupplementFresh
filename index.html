Tips 🛡️

Sleep Checklist 🌙

  • Take Magnesium with dinner to relax muscles (200-400 mg, glycinate preferred).
  • Try Melatonin 1-3 mg 30 minutes before bed for short-term use (NIH).
  • Use Valerian Root 300-600 mg if needed, but watch for grogginess.
  • Pair L-Theanine 100-200 mg with caffeine for calm focus.

Joint Health 💪

  • Combine Glucosamine 1500 mg with Chondroitin for arthritis relief.
  • Take Turmeric 500-1000 mg with a meal containing fat for absorption.
