Notes 📝

Sleep Checklist 🌙

  • Take Magnesium with dinner to relax muscles.
  • Try Melatonin before bed for short-term use.
  • Use Valerian Root if needed, but watch for grogginess.
  • Pair L-Theanine with caffeine for calm focus.

Joint Health 💪

  • Combine Glucosamine with Chondroitin for arthritis relief.
  • Take Turmeric with a meal containing fat for absorption.
